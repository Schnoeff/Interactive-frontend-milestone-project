Cornwall

A little bit about Cornwall

Holidays in Cornwall mean following in the footsteps of giants, kings and wizards, walking across oceans to castles and relaxing by harbours where seals pop up from the sea. Cornwall is a stunning peninsula in the South West of England with mile upon mile of craggy coastline, stretches of golden sand and clear waters with isles and islets standing proudly overlooking the mainland. The county is studded with English Heritage sites, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and National Trust properties as well as stunning gardens and attractions. From the amazing surfing beaches of the North Coast to the sheltered estuaries and inlets of the South, Cornwall holidays provide an ideal break in a region noted for some of the mildest weather in Britain.
