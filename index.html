About us

If you’re finding it difficult to choose your perfect seaside retreat, we have taken the time to place our rooms into small collections of our favourite destinations to make it easier for you . Whether you’re after a luxuary dog-friendly room in Brighton, a relaxing spa filled weekend in Bournemouth, or breathtaking views and towns in Cornwall, Seaside Retreats has all of the best reviewed hotels for each destination waiting just for you. With a wide array of luxurious rooms in Cornwall, Weymouth, Brighton, Bournemouth, Scarborough, The Isle of Wight and west Sussex we have a good idea on how a seaside retreat should be experienced.
